## Cross-Protocol Themes Observed

1. **DeFiLlama audit-registry blind spot.** Four of 15 protocols (Sanctum, Jupiter Lend, Kinetiq, Jupiter Perp) show `audits: 0` on DeFiLlama despite real audits existing. Teams do not submit reports to the registry. Automated risk dashboards under-count true coverage.
2. **Opaque multisig thresholds remain industry-wide.** Multisig `N/M` threshold is UNVERIFIED from public sources in the majority of protocols. On-chain verification sometimes resolves this (USDT0 confirmed 3/5 on Etherscan), but most teams do not document governance authority clearly. This is the single weakest disclosure dimension in the batch.
3. **Short timelocks on high-TVL protocols.** Lombard LBTC operates with a 1-hour upgrade timelock on $770M. Industry standard is 24-48h; best-in-class (Lido, Sky, Aave) is 4-12 days.
4. **New Solana lending at scale with audit-coverage gap.** Jupiter Lend has the most audits of any new Solana lender but its public surface makes it look under-audited. Information asymmetry is itself a risk signal.
5. **250x perps with 0% insurance fund.** Jupiter Perpetual offers up to 250x leverage against a pool with no dedicated insurance. At 250x, a 0.4% adverse move wipes a position.
6. **RWA audit publication falls short of DeFi norms.** BlackRock BUIDL has no public audit report for a $3B+ tokenized Treasury. Peers in the same category (Ondo, Circle USYC) publish more. Regulatory recourse via SEC-registered transfer agent partially compensates, but institutional brand does not substitute for public security disclosure.
7. **CEX-operated LSDs are structurally centralized.** Binance WBETH matches 5-6 of 7 Drift-type governance red flags -- not because Binance is malicious, but because any CEX-operated LSD is by design single-entity-controlled.
8. **Risk curators face real loss events.** Steakhouse Financial's March 2026 Resolv USR exposure demonstrated that "reputable curator" status does not prevent depositor losses. Allocator reallocation power (instant, without depositor consent) is a real attack surface.
